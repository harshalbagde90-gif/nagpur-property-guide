

# Nagpur Property Expert - Single-Page Landing Website

A modern, high-conversion landing page for a Nagpur-based real estate channel partner targeting local and out-of-city property investors.

---

## Design Foundation

**Visual Style:**
- Clean white/off-white background inspired by Housing.com
- Deep green primary color (trust + growth, complements WhatsApp green)
- Soft shadows on cards, generous white space
- Large, readable typography
- Mobile-first responsive design

---

## Page Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Compelling headline: "Invest in Verified Nagpur Properties with Complete Peace of Mind"
- Subheadline emphasizing registry-clear plots and local expertise
- Two prominent CTAs:
  - "Get Details on WhatsApp" (green WhatsApp button)
  - "Book Site Visit" (secondary button)
- Clean, professional imagery of Nagpur skyline or property

### 2. Trust Indicators Bar
- Four icon-based trust badges:
  - Registry-verified projects
  - On-ground site visits
  - Transparent process
  - Out-of-city buyer support
- Horizontal layout on desktop, 2x2 grid on mobile

### 3. Featured Properties (3-4 Cards)
Sample Nagpur localities:
- Wardha Road area plots
- Manish Nagar residential
- Besa-Pipla township
- Butibori industrial area

Each card includes:
- Area name with approximate location
- Starting price range (₹15L–25L format)
- Tags (Investment / Ready for Registry / RERA Approved)
- "View Details on WhatsApp" CTA

### 4. Out-of-City Buyer Section
Headline: "Buy Property in Nagpur — Even If You're Not Here"
- Highlights remote support services:
  - WhatsApp consultations
  - Live video site visits
  - Document guidance
  - Registry coordination
- Reassuring messaging for Mumbai/Pune investors

### 5. Why Invest in Nagpur
Educational content (not salesy):
- Affordable compared to metro cities
- Infrastructure: Metro, MIHAN, Outer Ring Road
- Growing IT corridor
- Long-term appreciation potential
- Presented with simple icons and factual tone

### 6. Process Steps
Visual step-by-step journey:
1. Share your requirement via WhatsApp
2. Get personalized shortlist from local expert
3. Site visit (in-person or video)
4. Documentation & registry support

Clean timeline or numbered cards design

### 7. Why Choose Us
Key differentiators:
- AI-powered follow-ups (never miss a message)
- Local Nagpur presence & knowledge
- Honest guidance, no fake urgency
- Long-term resale perspective
- Trusted by 100+ families (or placeholder)

### 8. Nagpur-Focused FAQ
Accordion-style questions:
- Can I buy Nagpur property while living in Mumbai/Pune?
- Is an in-person site visit mandatory?
- How does plot registry work in Maharashtra?
- What documents should I verify before buying?
- Is Nagpur good for long-term investment?

### 9. Final CTA Section
- Strong reassurance headline: "Ready to Take the First Step?"
- Simple lead form:
  - Name
  - Phone number
  - Budget range (dropdown)
  - Optional: "I'm calling from outside Nagpur" checkbox
- Large WhatsApp button as alternative
- Form designed to be webhook-ready for automation

---

## Functional Elements

### Sticky WhatsApp Button
- Fixed position on mobile (bottom-right)
- Always accessible during scroll
- Pre-filled message: "Hi, I'm interested in Nagpur property. Please share details."

### Dual Contact Options
- Quick WhatsApp button for instant connection
- Detailed form for those who prefer email/callback

### Mobile-First Design
- Touch-friendly CTAs
- Optimized card layouts
- Fast loading, minimal animations

### Automation Ready
- Forms structured for webhook integration (Zapier/Make compatible)
- Clean data capture for CRM/AI follow-up systems

---

## Content Tone
- Professional yet approachable
- Indian English, locally relevant
- Educational rather than pushy
- Builds trust through transparency
